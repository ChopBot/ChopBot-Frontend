About ChopBot

ChopBot is a Discord bot developed and maintained by Rasmus Gerdin, Michael Cumbers & Killian Higgins. The project started back in August of 2016, and has gona through many stages of development.

The bot itself features a multitude of features, the number of which is ever-growing as we take suggestions and requests from the servers that use the bot.

ChopBot is currently used on the following servers:

  • Cow Chop Community Discord
  • Kinda Funny Discord
  • Achievement Hunter Community Discord
  • g1 Family Discord
  • Tiny Meat Gang Patrons Discord

Our goal

We aim to provide a reliable, trustworthy Discord bot with a good set of moderation tools for your use. Whether you want to ban someone from entering voice channels, or send a formal warning, ChopBot has got you covered.

Need commands for users to have fun with? Worry not, we have that as well. Ask our magic 8-ball any question you'd like, flip a simple coin, or check when you joined the server you're in.

Get ChopBot

If you run a Rooster Teeth-related Discord server, and you'd like to use ChopBot, get in touch with us!

Got any more questions? Want to tell us what you think of the bot? Be sure to email us at help@chopbot.xyz.

You can also contact us on Discord by DMing rasmus#0001, stickmanmeyhem#8609 or Killian#4384.
