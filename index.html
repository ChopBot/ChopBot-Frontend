About ChopBot

ChopBot is a Discord bot developed by Rasmus Gerdin and Michael Cumbers. The project started back in August of 2016, and has gone through many stages of development.

The bot itself features a multitude of features, the number of which is ever-expanding as we take suggestions and requests from the servers that use the bot.

ChopBot is currently used on the following servers:

  • Cow Chop Community Discord
  • Kinda Funny Discord
  • Achievement Hunter Community Discord
  • Game Attack/ScrewAttack G1 Community Discord
